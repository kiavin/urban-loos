About Us

Urbanloos is a leading provider of mobile toilet services in Kenya, catering to the sanitation needs of events, construction sites, and more across Nakuru, Nyandarua, and Laikipia counties. We are committed to delivering exceptional service, ensuring every toilet unit is clean, well-stocked, and accompanied by a professional attendant at no extra cost. Our range of toilets, from Basic to VIP, ensures we have the right solution for every occasion and budget.
Our office is located on Kenyatta Avenue in Naivasha above Urbandining Restaurant.
